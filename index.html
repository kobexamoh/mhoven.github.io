Dr. Matt Hoven.

Hi, I'm Matt.

A professor who seriously considers sport, wonders about religion in the twenty-first century, and likes working with educators.

I do sport, religion, and education—admittedly, they’re not the first three things you’d put together. But it’s what I teach and write about: my hat-trick of sorts, my Trinity of interest, my rule of three. Sometimes I work with all three; other times, only one or two.

Variety piques my curiosity.

I work at St. Joseph’s College, University of Alberta, in Edmonton, Canada.
