I’m personable and professional. I want students to succeed--to learn the course content and let it impact your world. Call me “Matt” or “Dr. Hoven.”

Why take my courses?

  • I teach about interesting things.
  • Smaller class sizes and my commitment to engaging student learning means that you won't be bored in my classes.
  • I make it relevant.
  • I teach a lot of great people--you’ll like meeting them.
  • I trust that you want to learn; I encourage you to reflect and talk in class.
  • I work hard and put a lot into my classes.
  • I’m open to all students--you need not be a certain kind of student to take my classes or to do well.
  • My assignments are meaningful, connecting the course content to real life.
  • My assignments aren’t just academic papers--I assess through a variety of methods.
  • Course readings are manageable, interesting, and challenge your thinking.
  • I bring in guest speakers.
  • I get repeat offenders, along with relatives and friends of former students.

Because of what I teach at a Catholic College, we do talk about religion. I find it fascinating, especially because in public it often feels weird to do so. I teach from a Catholic, Christian perspective, yet also try to draw from other Christian traditions and even touch upon other religions. I like to hear about my students’ religious backgrounds--including religious nones. I can teach students better when I know more about them and their interests.

In short, my teaching is better than a slapshot to the face!
